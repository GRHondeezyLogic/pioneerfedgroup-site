NAICS 561210

Facilities Support Services

Federal facilities require consistent upkeep, coordination, and oversight to stay fully operational. Pioneer Federal Group provides facility support services that help agencies maintain safe, mission-ready environments through proactive management and dependable on-site support.

NAICS 236220

Construction & Renovation

From repairs and upgrades to larger improvement projects, we support construction requirements with disciplined planning and qualified resources. Our focus is delivering quality work that meets requirements on schedule, with minimal disruption to your operations.

NAICS 484230

Specialized Freight & Hazmat Transport

Moving regulated or sensitive materials requires more than transportation. It requires coordination, compliance, and accountability at every stage. We support agencies with secure logistics solutions designed to move critical materials safely and in full compliance.

NAICS 561730

Grounds Maintenance & Landscaping

Well-maintained grounds contribute to safety, professionalism, and operational readiness. We provide exterior maintenance services that keep federal properties presentable, accessible, and properly maintained throughout the year.

NAICS 484210

Moving & Relocation Services

Relocations involve more than moving boxes. They demand planning, coordination, and careful execution to prevent disruptions to personnel and operations. We support office moves, facility transitions, and equipment relocations with a focus on asset protection and accountability from start to finish.

NAICS 561990

General Support Services

Every mission has unique requirements. Pioneer Federal Group provides flexible support solutions tailored to agency needs, helping organizations access the right personnel, coordination, and operational support to accomplish their objectives.

How We Deliver

Work You Can Count On

Our delivery approach is built around one priority, reducing risk for contracting officers and program teams. From kickoff to closeout, we stay engaged, communicate early, and hold the work to the standard required.

  • Plan and Align. Before work begins, we review requirements, identify constraints, and build a clear execution plan. Everyone knows the scope, the schedule, and their role.
  • Execute and Monitor. We stay actively engaged throughout performance, overseeing all personnel and vendors to ensure work stays on track, on schedule, and in compliance.
  • Report and Adjust. We keep contracting officers informed with straightforward updates, surface issues before they compound, and make adjustments quickly to keep work moving forward.
