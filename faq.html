About Pioneer Federal Group

The basics.

What does Pioneer Federal Group do?

We handle facilities support, construction and repair, freight and hazmat transport, grounds maintenance, moving services, and general support work for federal agencies. One team handles the whole job from start to finish.

Who owns Pioneer Federal Group?

Desi Pelaez founded the company and owns 51 percent. He is a 100 percent disabled Marine Corps veteran, and he serves as CEO and Managing Member. Jonas Moreno co-founded the company, owns 49 percent, and serves as Chief Operating Officer.

Is Jonas a veteran too?

No. Jonas brings experience from trades, operations, and running his own business. Desi is the veteran, and he holds majority ownership and final say over company decisions. That ownership and control is what makes Pioneer Federal Group eligible for veteran-owned status.

Certifications, Explained Simply

What all the terms mean.

What does SDVOSB mean?

SDVOSB stands for Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business. It means a veteran with a service-connected disability owns most of the company and actually runs it, not just on paper. Pioneer Federal Group qualifies because Desi, a 100 percent disabled veteran, owns 51 percent and makes the final calls.

Is Pioneer Federal Group officially certified yet?

We already meet the ownership and control rules. The formal certification paperwork with the Small Business Administration is in progress.

What is a NAICS code?

A NAICS code is just a number the government uses to sort businesses by what kind of work they do. It helps agencies match a contract to the right company. Pioneer Federal Group holds codes for facilities support, construction, freight and hazmat transport, grounds maintenance, moving services, and general support.

What is SAM.gov, and are you registered?

SAM.gov is the government's official system for registering as a contractor. A company has to be listed there before it can bid on or win a federal contract. Our registration, along with our UEI and CAGE code, is currently in progress.

What is a capability statement?

It is a short summary of what a company does, what it is certified for, and how to reach it. Contracting officers use it to quickly check if a business is a good fit. You can view ours online, and the full document is available on request.

What is a Managing Member?

It is the person in charge of running an LLC and making final decisions. At Pioneer Federal Group, that is Desi Pelaez.
