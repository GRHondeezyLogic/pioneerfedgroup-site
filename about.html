About Us

Founded to Serve a Second Time.

Pioneer Federal Group was built on the belief that government agencies deserve contractors who take ownership of the mission. We are not driven by excuses. We are driven by execution.

Our Story

From aviation supply lines to federal task orders.

Pioneer Federal Group is a Georgia-based contracting company founded by a U.S. Marine Corps veteran whose six years of service spanned aviation supply, warehouse management, finance, and logistics — the operational backbone that keeps aircraft flying and missions moving.

That experience shapes how we contract: clear scope, disciplined coordination, and a single point of accountability. We built Pioneer to bring that standard to federal agencies across facilities, construction, transport, and general services — and to keep earning the trust that comes with the word "federal" in our name.
